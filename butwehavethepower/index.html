Union Strike Folk Song

Lisa: Come gather round, children, It's high time ye learned 'Bout a hero named Homer And a devil named Burns.

We'll march 'till we drop The girls and the fellas. We'll fight 'till the death Or else fold like umbrellas.

So we'll march day and night By the big cooling tower. They have the plant

But we have the power. Lenny: Now do "Classical Gas!"

So we'll march day and night By the big cooling tower. They have the plant But we have the power.
